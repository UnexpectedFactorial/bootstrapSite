Preamble

This build was a long time coming. I had started to paint this around 2021 with painting the chrome on some parts. Eventually, I had stopped working on this as finding the time and place to paint was difficult. After two long years and creating a miniture paint booth, I could continue what I started.

Paints Used

  • ALC 107 - Alclad Chrome
  • ALC 108 - Alclad Pale Gold
  • Molotow Liquid Chrome

  • Alclad Aqua Gloss
  • Vallejo Polyurethane Varnish - Matte
  • Vallejo Polyurethane Varnish - Satin
